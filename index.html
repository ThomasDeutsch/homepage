The Need For Integration

to prevent the division of labor in software development

One year ago, in January 2019, Chris Coyier released the article The Great Divide. It shows that frontend programming is now divided into sub-disciplines and that it leads to an identity crisis. I think that the problem we are facing reaches way beyond an identity crisis. We are not able to see a systemic problem - a cost that comes with every sub-discipline. It is the Voldemort problem - by splitting his soul he had lost his humanity and we are facing the same problem. Sub-disciplines are needed, but we dehumanize our workplace if we fail to see the cost: the need for integration!
